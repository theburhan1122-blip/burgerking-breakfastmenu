Customer Favorites

Top 6 Items You Need To Try

1

Bacon King

The undisputed champion. Two flame-grilled beef patties, a ridiculous amount of crispy bacon, melted American cheese, and mayo on a toasted sesame bun. This burger doesn’t ask for permission — it walks in and owns the room. One bite and you’ll understand why people drive out of their way for it. I still dream about the smoky bacon crunch.

2
2x BEEF

Double Whopper

Double the flame-grilled patties, double the juice running down your wrist. Fresh lettuce, ripe tomatoes, pickles, onions, and that signature Whopper sauce. It’s the burger that made Burger King famous for a reason — every bite tastes like summer cookouts. My personal “I had a rough day, fix it” order.

3

Impossible Whopper

Flame-grilled Impossible patty that bleeds, sears, and tastes terrifyingly close to the real thing. Same toppings, same sesame bun, zero meat. Even die-hard carnivores order this now. I watched my meat-loving dad finish one and whisper “do that again.”

4

Original Chicken Sandwich

Long, juicy, lightly breaded chicken fillet with crisp lettuce and creamy mayo on a toasted sesame bun. This sandwich has been perfect since the 90s and they’ve never messed with it. Pure comfort food that hits harder than it has any right to.

5

Spicy BK Royal Crispy

Crispy, juicy chicken fillet coated in spicy seasoning, topped with lettuce and tomato on a buttery potato bun. The heat builds perfectly — not too mild, not ghost-pepper stupid. It’s the sandwich I crave when I want flavor that punches back.

6

Chicken Fries

Crispy, white-meat chicken shaped like fries so you can dip them in literally every sauce. Zesty, BBQ, ranch — doesn’t matter. People buy these by the dozen and guard them like treasure. I’ve seen grown adults fight over the last one.
